I am an Assistant Professor at Dongguk University in Seoul, Korea. My research focuses on agentic AI and large language models, particularly how multimodal data (video, image, and time series) can enable autonomous reasoning and decision-making. I work on integrating perception models with LLM-driven agents for medical imaging, biomedical signal analysis, and clinical decision support.
